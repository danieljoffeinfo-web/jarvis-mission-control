# Jarvis Mission Control Manual

This dashboard is designed to feel simple, not technical.

What each area means:

1. Mission briefing
   - Type what you want Jarvis to do.
   - Example: "Review today's travel work and tell me the blockers."

2. Goals
   - Goals are outcomes you care about.
   - Example: "Be ready for tomorrow by 9am."

3. Jobs
   - Jobs are repeated tasks on a schedule.
   - Example: "Every weekday at 08:30, send me a priority summary."

4. Saved workflows
   - These are repeatable playbooks Jarvis can use for redeploys and recurring jobs.
   - You do not need to remember the steps yourself.

5. Activity
   - This is the recent history of what changed.
